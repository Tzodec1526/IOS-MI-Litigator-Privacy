MI Litigator — Privacy Policy

Effective July 18, 2026

MI Litigator is a reference app for Michigan and Federal court rules. It is built to work entirely on your device: there are no accounts, no sign-in, no ads, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. Your favorites, settings, and free-trial start date are stored only on your device and never leave it. The rules content is bundled with the app and works offline.

Subscriptions

Optional subscriptions are processed by Apple through your Apple ID; we never see your name, payment details, or Apple ID. To determine whether a subscription is active, the app uses RevenueCat, which processes an anonymous, randomly generated identifier and your App Store purchase receipt on our behalf. This information is not linked to your identity and is used solely to validate subscription status.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
