MI Litigator — Privacy Policy

Effective July 19, 2026

MI Litigator is a free reference app for Michigan and Federal court rules. It is built to work entirely on your device: there are no accounts, no sign-in, no ads, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs of any kind.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not collect, transmit, or sell any information. Your favorites and settings are stored only on your device and never leave it. The rules content and the on-device search models are bundled with the app, so everything — including every question you type into search — is processed entirely on your iPhone and works offline. The app makes no network requests.
